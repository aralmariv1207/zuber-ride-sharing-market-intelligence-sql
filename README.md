# Zuber: Ride-Sharing Market Intelligence

## About

Analyzes the Chicago ride-sharing market to help Zuber, a new competitor, understand passenger behavior. By processing data on top taxi companies and destination hotspots, and performing statistical tests on weather impacts, it provides insights to optimize market entry.

## Technical Highlights

· **SQL Data Retrieval**: Extracted and aggregated ride data using complex joins to link weather conditions with trip durations.

· **Market Benchmarking**: Identified the top 10 taxi companies in Chicago by ride volume and visualized trip distribution.

· **Statistical Validation**: Performed an independent T-test to confirm that rain significantly increases the average duration of rides from the Loop to O'Hare.
